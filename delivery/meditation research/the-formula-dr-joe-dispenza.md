## Lesson 2 — The Model of Transformation

The average person thinks 60,000–70,000 thoughts per day. 90% are the same as the day before.

**The cycle:** Same thoughts → same choices → same actions → same experiences → same emotions → same thoughts.

**Evolution:** New information → new thoughts → new choices → new behaviours → new experiences → new feelings → new thoughts.

### Your Personality Creates Your Personal Reality

- Personality = how you think + how you act + how you feel
- Most people try to change their reality without changing themselves — it doesn't work

### Neurological Networks

- Neurons that fire together, wire together
- Repetition creates hardwired neurological patterns → identity
- By age 35, **95% of who you are is unconscious, memorised programs**
- Change requires entering the subconscious mind

### The Loop of Thinking and Feeling

- Thoughts produce chemicals → you feel what you think
- You then think the way you feel → a loop
- **Thoughts = language of the brain. Feelings = language of the body.**
- Together they create your state of being

### To Change Is To Be Greater Than:

1. **Your environment** — stop letting external circumstances control your thoughts and feelings
2. **Your body** — break the thinking-feeling loop and unconscious habituated programs
3. **Time** — stop living in the familiar past or predictable future; enter the present moment

To create brain coherence: get beyond your identity, personality, and familiar thoughts — and linger as awareness in nothing known.

### Brain Wave States

| State | Frequency | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **High Beta** | 22–50 cycles/sec | Emergency mode, survival chemicals, cannot learn or create |
| **Mid Beta** | 15–22 cycles/sec | Vigilant and aroused, attention on outer environment |
| **Low Beta** | 13–15 cycles/sec | Relaxed, concentrating, rational thinking — healthy state |
| **Alpha** | 8–13 cycles/sec | Inner world focus, imagination, light meditative state, creative |
| **Theta** | 4–8 cycles/sec | Half awake/asleep, door to subconscious wide open, reprogrammable |
| **Delta** | 0.5–4 cycles/sec | Deep sleep, body restores itself |

By slowing brain waves through meditation, you move past the analytical mind and can rewrite subconscious programs.

- The entire body begins to return to homeostasis

When you remove all bodies, people, objects, planets, stars, and galaxies — all you're left with is infinite black space. This is the void / vacuum / quantum field. It is filled with frequency, energy, and information — the wavefunction in quantum physics.
