# Olly's Meditation Process
**Based on:** Dr Joe Dispenza — The Formula + Example Meditation 1
**Purpose:** Creating a new reality / reprogramming the body to believe a future has already happened
**Last updated:** 2026-05-20

---

## Intentions

**Main Intention:** Health

**Sub-Intentions:**
1. Back working again
2. Running most days
3. Visiting friends all over the world

---

## The Practice

### Step 1 — Settle and Relax

- Take a few deep, relaxing breaths
- With each exhale, let go of any tension in the body
- Allow yourself to arrive fully in the moment

---

### Step 2 — Heart-Focused Breathing

- Begin to place your attention on your heart
- Breathe a little slower and deeper than normal
- Imagine the air coming in and out of your heart
- Let this continue until you feel settled and open

---

### Step 3 — Bring Up the Main Intention

- Bring to mind the letter or symbol you've chosen to represent **Health**
- Hold it lightly — it's the anchor for everything that follows

---

### Step 4 — Work Through Each Sub-Intention

Do this process for each sub-intention in turn:

**1. Create the Image**
Ask yourself the frontal lobe question — for example:
- *"What would back working again look like?"*

Let your mind go through its natural process. Don't force it. Let the image build from your own knowledge, memories, and references. Really get into it — where are you, what are you doing, what does that version of you look like?

**2. Step Into the Feeling**
Once the image feels comfortable, ask yourself:
- *"What would this feel like?"*

Get into the emotions of it. Let them rise naturally. Breathe that emotion slowly in and out of your heart. Stay here for as long as feels right.

**3. Lightly Release**
Once you've felt it, simply let it go. No need to hold on. Just a quiet internal sense of — *it's done, I trust it* — then move on.

### Step 5 — Return

When you've moved through all three sub-intentions, take a few gentle breaths and slowly bring your awareness back to the room. Open your eyes when you're ready.

---

## Notes

- If the emotion doesn't come easily, don't force it — start with gratitude and let it build from there
- The image comes first, the feeling follows — let the question do the work
- Each session is conditioning your body a little closer to believing this is already real
- Come back and note what's working and what isn't so we can adjust
