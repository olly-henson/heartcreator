# Example Meditation 1 — Dr Joe Dispenza
**Type:** Breathwork + Space Awareness + Tuning In to New Potentials

---

## Part 1 — Breathwork (Energy Ascension)

*Using your body as an instrument of consciousness to ascend your energy and free your body from the chains of the past. To breathe with an intensity of energy that's greater than the addiction of the emotions. To no longer let your body be your mind.*

**The breath cycle (repeat multiple times):**

1. **Contract** the intrinsic muscles — perineum, lower abdomen, upper abdomen
2. **Inhale** through your nose simultaneously, drawing energy upward like fluid up a straw — from the perineum, up through the lower abdomen, upper abdomen, chest, throat, brain, to the **top of your head**
3. **Hold** the energy at the top of your head — squeeze and hold, allowing your awareness to follow the energy
4. **Exhale** and relax completely

**Intention behind the breath:**
- Pull the mind out of the body
- Move from survival to creation
- Free yourself from the past
- Go from particle to wave, from matter to energy
- Create an electromagnetic field around the body

## Part 2 — Sensing Space (Body Awareness → Open Focus)

*Moving attention through different centres of the body, sensing the space each occupies — then expanding awareness beyond it.*

**Chest / Sternum**
- Become aware of the space behind your sternum
- Sense the volume of space that the centre of your chest occupies
- Expand awareness to the volume of space beyond your shoulders, around your torso

**Throat / Neck**
- Rest your attention in the space your throat occupies
- Feel the volume of space your throat occupies
- Sense the volume of space around your neck, beyond your throat

**Back of the Head**
- Ascend awareness to the space the back of your head occupies
- Become aware of the volume of space the back of your head occupies
- Feel the volume of space beyond your head, around this place in all of space

**Centre of the Head**
- Move awareness to the space the centre of your head occupies
- Sense the volume of space the centre of your head takes up
- Feel the energy of space beyond your head, the volume of space around your head

## Part 3 — Becoming Nobody (Entering the Field)

*Moving consciousness from the known to the unknown.*

- From **somebody** to **nobody**
- From **someone** to **no one**
- From **something** to **nothing**
- From **somewhere** to **nowhere**
- From **some time** to **no time**
- From **space and time** to **beyond space and time**

Become an awareness in the infinite field of all possibilities. Invest your energy into the unknown. The longer you rest your attention in the unknown, the more you bring possibility to you. To create by thought alone means you have to become a thought alone.

---

## Part 4 — Tuning In to New Potentials

*Done twice — once for a first potential, once for a second.*

**For each potential:**

1. Recall the potential you want to tune in to — something that already exists in the quantum field
2. Change your energy — move into a new state of being
3. Broadcast a new electromagnetic signature into the field
4. Open your heart and **teach your body emotionally what it would feel like to be in that future now**
5. Allow your body to respond to a new mind
6. From this new state of being — **live that future now:**
   - What choices will you make?
   - What behaviours will you demonstrate?
   - What experiences await you?
   - How do they feel?
7. **Surrender** your creation to a greater mind — allow it to organise in a way that's right for you
8. Return to the infinite field — nobody, no one, nothing, nowhere, no time

> *"Whatever you experience in the realm beyond space and time and emotionally embrace must manifest in this space and time. It's the law."*

---

## Part 5 — The Blessing

*Place your left hand over your heart.*

Bless:
- **Your body** — that it responds to a new mind
- **Your life** — with a new energy, that it be a reflection of your mind
- **Your future** — that it be filled with adventure
- **Your past** — that it turn to wisdom
- **Your soul** — that its fire burn brightly within you and speak to you in infinite ways
- **The power within you** — that it move closer to you, stir in you, move through you and all around you, and show evidence that it is real in your life

## Part 6 — Gratitude and Return

> *"If the thought sends the signal out and the feeling draws the event back — open your heart and give thanks for a new life."*

- The emotional signature of **gratitude** means you accept it has already happened
- The longer you rest in gratitude, the more you are a magnet to a new destiny
- **Gratitude is the ultimate state of receivership**
- Get up from your meditation as if your prayers are already answered

**Return:**
Bring your awareness back to a new body, a new environment, and a whole new time. When you're ready, open your eyes.
