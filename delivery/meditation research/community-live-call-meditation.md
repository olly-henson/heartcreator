# Community Live Call Meditation
**For:** Free community weekly live call (taster)
**Intention:** Safety
**Last updated:** 2026-05-20

---

## Your Script (What to Say Out Loud)

---

### Step 1 — Settle and Relax

*"I want you to close your eyes and take a few deep, relaxing breaths. With each exhale, let go of any tension you're holding in your body. Just allow yourself to arrive here fully."*

---

### Step 2 — Heart-Focused Breathing

*"Now begin to bring your attention to your heart. Breathe a little slower and a little deeper than normal. And imagine that the air is coming in and out of your heart. Just let yourself settle here for a moment."*

---

### Step 3 — Introduce the Intention

*"I want you to hold one word in your mind — Safety. Just let that word sit there lightly. Don't overthink it, just hold it."*

---

### Step 4 — Create the Image

*"Now when you feel ready, ask yourself — what would safety look like for me? Let your mind go through its own process. Don't force anything. Just let the image come — where are you, what does it look like, what does that version of your life look like? Really get into it."*

*[Pause — give them time to build the image]*

---

### Step 5 — Step Into the Feeling

*"And once you're there — once you can see it — ask yourself, what would this feel like? Let that feeling rise. And as it does, breathe it slowly in and out of your heart. Stay here for as long as feels right."*

*[Pause — give them time to feel it]*

---

### Step 6 — Return

*"When you're ready, take a few gentle breaths and slowly begin to bring your awareness back to your body and back to the room. And when you feel ready, gently open your eyes."*

---

## Notes for Olly

- Keep pauses generous — people need more time than you think
- Don't rush Step 4 to Step 5 — let the image fully form first
- Your tone should stay calm and unhurried throughout
- This is a taster — the goal is for them to feel something, not to understand the full framework
