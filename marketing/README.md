# Olly Henson Coaching — AI Operating System

> Built for CEO Olly Henson by the AI for Impact Hub OS Builder.

## What This Is

This is your personalised AI operating system — a set of markdown files that teach any AI assistant to work like you. Your voice. Your audience. Your rules. Your business context.

It's not a chatbot. It's not an app. It's a portable, platform-neutral system that works with whatever AI tools you already use — Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, or anything else.

---

## The 6 Components

Your AI operating system is built from exactly 6 components:

| # | Component | File(s) | What It Does |
|---|-----------|---------|--------------|
| 1 | **The Brain** | `AGENT.md` | Your business context, brand voice, rules, and ethics. The master instruction set. |
| 2 | **The Skills** | `skills/*.md` | One file per task. Each skill teaches the AI how to do a specific job. |
| 3 | **The Memory** | `memory/*.md` | Persistent context that accumulates over time. Content logs, performance data, audience insights. |
| 4 | **The Connections** | `connections.md` | Your tech stack mapped to AI integration points. |
| 5 | **The Rules** | `governance.md` | What the AI can do automatically vs what needs your approval. Safety boundaries. |
| 6 | **The Rhythm** | `schedule.md` | When things happen. Daily, weekly, monthly automations. |

## The 3 Tiers

Your files are organised into three tiers. Start with Starter and level up as you're ready.

### Starter Pack (Levels 1-2) — Start Here
- **AGENT.md** — your business brain
- **skills/** — your marketing skill files
- **QUICK_START.md** — setup instructions
- **README.md** — this file

Works immediately by pasting files into any AI chat. Five minutes from download to first use.

### Connected Pack (Levels 3-4)
Everything from Starter, plus:
- **connections.md** — tech stack integrations
- **governance.md** — auto vs ask rules
- **schedule.md** — weekly rhythm and automations
- **memory/** — content log, performance, calendar, audience insights

Your AI now reads from and writes to your real tools.

### Full OS Pack (Level 5)
Everything from Connected, plus:
- **org-chart.md** — your AI department structure

Multiple departments, orchestration, and a bird's-eye view of your AI operations.

## How to Use

1. Read `QUICK_START.md` for platform-specific setup instructions
2. Load `AGENT.md` into your AI tool of choice
3. Reference skill files when you need the AI to do specific tasks
4. As you grow, add Connected and Full OS files

## Updating Your OS

Your business evolves. Your OS should too. Re-run the intake interview at any time to regenerate your files with updated information. Your memory files persist — they capture what you've learned along the way.
