## Blog Post Structure

### Content Structure
1. **H1 Title** — includes primary keyword
2. **Hook paragraph** — 2-3 sentences establishing the problem for People suffering with chronic symptoms who want to get their life back
3. **H2 sections** (3-5) — clear, scannable headers
4. **Body paragraphs** — 2-4 sentences each, conversational but authoritative
5. **Lists** — for steps, comparisons, key points
6. **Conclusion / CTA** — tie back to main point, link to newsletter or socials

### Length
800-1,200 words per post — enough depth to be useful, short enough to finish in one sitting.

## SEO Rules

- Include primary keyword in: title, first paragraph, 2+ H2 headers, meta description, slug
- 2-3 secondary keywords used naturally throughout
- Use question-format headers where appropriate
- Link to 1-2 previous blog posts + 1-2 external sources
- Title tag: 50-60 characters | Meta description: 150-160 characters

---

## AI-Search Optimisation

- Lead paragraphs with definitive statements that can be directly quoted
- Use **Term:** Definition formatting
- Create numbered step-by-step processes
- Include comparison tables
- Cite sources, studies, or real-world examples
- Demonstrate first-hand experience ("In our work with People suffering with chronic symptoms who want to get their life back...")
