## 6. Voice and Structure Rules

### The writing style â€” non-negotiable

- **Short lines.** Single sentences as their own paragraph. Creates pace and pulls the reader down.
- **Ellipsis (...)** used deliberately to create forward momentum and open loops. Not overused.
- **Plain language throughout.** No clinical terms, no wellness jargon, no corporate language.
- **British English** â€” spelling, grammar, phrasing throughout.
- **First person, conversational** â€” sounds like a message from someone who has been through it.
- **Never preachy.** Never "you deserve this." Never aspirational wellness-influencer tone.
- **Personal story is the primary credibility anchor** â€” not credentials.
- **Rhetorical questions** used to maintain momentum.
- **Reframe blame before offering solution** â€” "it's not your fault" and "it's not an information problem" remove self-blame and open the prospect to the real cause.
- **Manage expectations honestly** â€” "it is going to take learning and consistent practice" builds credibility.

## 11. Email Broadcast Campaigns

Separate from the nurture sequence. Broadcast emails are one-off sends to a wider list — typically repurposed from YouTube Community Posts or other content. Stored in the **Email Broadcast Campaigns** Notion database (`36e30e58-6a0d-811c-b455-ce8fe3da15be`).

### Database Fields

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| Subject Line | The above-the-fold hook — maps from the community post Title |
| Email Content | The body copy — adapted from the community post, platform language removed |
| CTA | Inline hyperlinked text — see format below |
| Type of Email | Select (Personal Story, Belief-Changing, etc.) |
| Status | Draft → Ready → Scheduled |
| Date | Send date |

### Syncing from YouTube Community Posts

When a community post is synced to Email Broadcast Campaigns:

1. **Strip all platform-specific language** — any line containing "comments below" must be removed. That language is for YouTube. It reads as a mistake in an email.
2. **Do not copy the community post CTA verbatim** — "I've dropped a free Heart Activation Meditation in the comments below for you" has no equivalent in email. Remove it and use the email CTA format instead.

### CTA Format — Non-Negotiable

The meditation CTA in email is always inline hyperlinked text, never a plain URL:

```
You can download the free Heart Activation Meditation ===> HERE
```

Where **HERE** is hyperlinked to the meditation URL with email-specific UTM parameters.

Approved:
```
You can download the free Heart Activation Meditation ===> HERE
[HERE links to: https://ollyhenson.com/meditation?utm_source=email&utm_medium=broadcast&utm_campaign=meditation]
```

Rejected:
```
👉 Download the free Heart Activation Meditation here: https://ollyhenson.com/meditation?utm_source=youtube&utm_medium=community&utm_campaign=meditation
```
Why rejected: wrong UTM (community not email), plain URL not hyperlinked text.

### UTM Links — Email vs Community

Every channel gets its own UTM. Never copy a community post UTM into an email.

| Channel | utm_source | utm_medium |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| YouTube Community Post | youtube | community |
| Email Broadcast | email | broadcast |
| YouTube Description | youtube | description |
| YouTube Pinned Comment | youtube | pinned_comment |
