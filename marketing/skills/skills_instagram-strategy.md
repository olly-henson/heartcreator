# Instagram Strategy — CEO Olly Henson

> Generated from onboarding data. Review quarterly as the algorithm evolves.

## Caption Rules

- **Always start with the HEART CTA** — every Instagram Reel caption must begin with: `➡️ Comment "HEART" below and I'll send you my free meditation that will help you keep feeling your new reality as real, so it happens.` The caption body follows after. CTA is always first, never last.
- **No em-dashes or hyphens as clause separators** — they read as AI-generated. Use a full stop instead.
- **Limit hyphens overall** — if a sentence needs a hyphen to connect two thoughts, rewrite it as two sentences.
- **One idea per paragraph** — short, punchy lines read better on mobile.
- **CTA always last** — comment HEART or link in bio, never mid-caption.

## Reels Strategy

### Length Sweet Spots
- **5-7 seconds:** Caption-driven, text overlay
- **15-30 seconds:** Discovery sweet spot
- **30-60 seconds:** Deeper education for engaged followers
- **Avoid:** Over 90 seconds

### The 1.7-Second Rule
The first 1.7 seconds determine whether someone keeps watching. Your hook must be immediate.

### Script Templates

**Quick Tip (15-20s):**
```
[0-3s] HOOK: Bold text — "[Topic] tip most People suffering with long-term chronic illness or chronic conditions who are looking to return back to living a normal life miss"
[3-8s] CONTEXT: "If you're dealing with Overwhelm..."
[8-15s] THE TIP: Actionable advice
[15-20s] CTA: "Save this" or "Follow @theollyhenson"
```

**Story + Lesson (30-45s):**
```
[0-3s] HOOK: "Something happened that changed how I think about [topic]"
[3-12s] THE STORY: Quick, specific anecdote
[12-25s] TURNING POINT: What changed
[25-35s] THE LESSON: What I learned
[35-45s] CTA: "What's your experience? Comment below"
```
