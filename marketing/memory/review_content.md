## Rachel — Who We're Working With (Cross-Book Summary)

This is the pattern that emerges consistently across every book in this file. Use this as the working picture of Rachel when creating any content.

**She is not a beginner.** She's been in self-help and spirituality for years — often a decade or more. She's read Tolle, Dispenza, Abraham Hicks, Neville Goddard. She's done therapy, affirmations, visualisation boards, EFT, meditation. She understands the concepts well enough to explain them to other people.

**She believes in reality creation. It still doesn't work for her.** That gap is the specific wound that makes her ready for Olly's work. She can't figure out what she's doing wrong because intellectually she's doing everything right. The frustration isn't doubt — it's bafflement. She knows this should work. She's tried everything. And the thing that needs to move hasn't moved.

**Her body is often breaking down.** A striking pattern across these reviews: chronic illness that medicine couldn't explain or fix. Migraines since childhood. Autoimmune conditions. Gut issues. Fatigue. She already suspects the link between suppressed emotion and physical symptoms — she just hasn't found a way through it.

**She carries a shame layer that most content misses.** She doesn't just hold fear and grief. She holds shame about still being stuck after this long. Shame about feeling jealous, unworthy or angry when she "knows better." That second layer of suppression is what makes the emotional residue so hard to shift — and what makes the positive thinking approach actively counterproductive. She's been trying to install new beliefs on top of shame she hasn't been allowed to feel.

**The missing piece isn't more knowledge.** She has plenty. What she hasn't found is a way to feel the new reality as real in the body. Every book that gets rave reviews in this niche offers some version of that — a mechanism for moving the emotional body, not just the mind. That is exactly what Olly's programme provides.

**The through-line:** more reading, more understanding, more technique — none of it is the answer. The answer is feeling. And that is the entire argument for the Heart Creator Programme.
