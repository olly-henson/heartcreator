# Funnel Overview — Olly Henson Coaching

> The strategic context for all content creation. Every piece of content has a job in this funnel. Read this before planning any content calendar or writing any copy.

---

## The goal

Move the right people from content → free meditation → email nurture → application for The Heart Creator Program.

---

## Entry points

There are three ways someone enters the funnel:

1. **YouTube** — watches content, clicks meditation link or applies directly
2. **Instagram** — watches a Reel, comments HEART, ManyChat delivers the meditation
3. **Skool** — discovers the community through Skool's own discovery/search as cold traffic

---

## Platform roles

### YouTube (main channel)

**Job:** Make Rachel feel heard and understood. The concept clicks. She sees the logical next step.

**Content tone:** Soft, educational, argument-led. Full case built over 10–20 minutes.

**CTAs:**
- Soft dual CTA in the video itself — meditation download and coaching application
- Description has both links above the fold
- Pinned comment also has both links

**Next step for viewer:** Download the free Heart Activation Meditation (`/meditation`) or apply directly for The Heart Creator Program if they've consumed enough content to be ready.

**YouTube Community Posts:**
- Sporadically link to the meditation in comments (more often)
- Occasionally link to the coaching program in comments (less often)

---

### Instagram

**Job:** Hook people in. Make them feel heard. Drive meditation downloads.

**Content tone:** Punchy, direct, hook-led. 60–90 seconds. Awareness and recognition first.

**Primary CTA:** Comment HEART → ManyChat delivers the meditation link automatically

**Stories CTA:** "DM me the word HEART" → triggers ManyChat funnel

**Profile:**
- Link to meditation in bio
- Link to Heart Creator Program in bio

**Note:** Instagram CTA is always the meditation first. The program is available via the profile but not the primary CTA from Reels.

---

### Skool Community — Heart Creator DIY Program

**Job:** Serve two audiences:
1. **Warm leads** who went through the email sequence, couldn't afford the 1-2-1, and are offered this as a downsell
2. **Cold discovery traffic** finding the page organically through Skool's own platform

**Pricing:**
- $997/year
- 7-day free trial included

**Page requirement:** Must work for both audiences. Cold traffic will land here with no prior context — the page needs to be self-contained, clear and compelling on its own.

## Key rules for content creation

- Every piece of content must have a clear job in this funnel — awareness, education, or conversion
- YouTube and Instagram Reels always drive to the meditation first (except warm/ready audiences)
- The meditation is the primary lead magnet — protect it as the main CTA
- The program can be referenced in YouTube descriptions, pinned comments and Instagram profile — not forced as the primary CTA on cold content
- Skool page must work as a standalone for cold traffic — never assume prior context
