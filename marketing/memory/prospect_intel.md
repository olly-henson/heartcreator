### 2026-07-14 — Instagram DM

**Source:** Instagram DM, unprompted (prospect messaged first)

**Raw:**
> "I need help with my manifestations"
> [Olly] "Tell me more about what you need help with"
> "Thank you for actually replying. I feel stuck. I understand manifestations scientifically and the more I read about it from a neurological or physical aspect, the more I understand and I have been practicing what feels like every method under the sun and absolutely nothing is shifting"

**Avatar match:** Segment 2 — the sharper, prioritised sub-segment (see [[project_heart_creator_audience]]). She explicitly names the gap: intellectual/scientific understanding is high, method-hopping is exhausted ("every method under the sun"), and nothing is shifting. This is not a segment 1 "why isn't this working" prospect — she already understands the mechanism at a knowledge level and is still stuck, which is the exact gap the Heart-Opening Meditation argument targets (understanding vs feeling).

**Strong enough to update argument sheet/avatar:** Yes — reinforces the existing Actual Cause / Current Belief framing, doesn't require a new addition, but is a strong verbatim quote worth using directly.

**Content angle:** Proven phrase candidate for hooks/titles: *"absolutely nothing is shifting"* and *"every method under the sun"* — both are high-resonance, verbatim prospect language for segment 2 titles (e.g. "You understand manifestation scientifically. Still nothing is shifting."). Also strong evidence that DMs replying personally (not just automated CTA) can surface highly qualified intel — worth noting as a source, not just for content but for the DM response funnel itself.

**Follow-up detail (same conversation, same day):**

> Background: "I lived an exciting fulfilling life. Exciting fast paced job, long term relationship etc. opportunities always fell in my lap. Then it all fell apart. I went on a holiday came back, lost my job and relationship in the same month. The only thing that got me through it was the belief that everything happens for a reason, better things were coming etc. I genuinely believed it and embodied it and was ready and excited for anything that was to come."
>
> Result: "nothing really came. I got a new job that is more stable and secure but that's about it. No new opportunities, relationships, friendships none of that so I don't believe my nervous system is in fight or flight it's more in a coma with just absolutely no movement"
>
> On root cause: "I used to think it was my limiting beliefs. I spent my entire life preparing for the worst case scenario and being pleasantly surprised if anything good ever happened so the notion of doing the opposite and expecting the best to happen or the concept of my thoughts actually shaping my reality was really enlightening so I have consciously been practicing this for almost two years and I'm seeing less results now than ever before"
>
> Current creation goal: "A meaningful relationship"
>
> Creative practice (Olly asked her to walk through it): "I am visualizing, affirming (tried every form of affirmations verbal or written) practicing gratitude, writing 10 things I am grateful for every day. Living in the wish fulfillment and feeling the feelings now."

**Why this matters — two things stand out:**
1. **She self-diagnoses "coma, no movement" and explicitly rules out fight or flight — but Olly's read (2026-07-14) is she's wrong about that self-diagnosis: her nervous system is in fight or flight, she just doesn't recognise it as that.** Correction to an earlier draft of this note: this is NOT evidence to shift content language away from fight-or-flight or toward a "frozen/coma" framing — don't build titles around "your nervous system isn't in fight or flight, it's frozen," that framing was rejected. The real lesson is that prospects can misdiagnose their own nervous system state entirely (label it "coma" when it's actually still survival/fight-or-flight), which is itself useful content territory (people not recognising fight-or-flight because it doesn't look like classic panic) but should be framed as *still fight-or-flight, just unrecognised* — not as a different state
2. **Textbook segment 2, high technique fluency, declining results over time** ("seeing less results now than ever before" after ~2 years of conscious practice). She's doing everything right by the book (visualisation, every form of affirmation, daily gratitude list, wish fulfillment, feeling the feelings now) — this is a strong real-world proof case that technique execution isn't the gap. This is the angle Olly confirmed for content: "you're doing every technique right, that's not why nothing's shifting" / "more affirmations, more visualising, less happening."
