# Preferences — Olly Henson Coaching

> How Olly and the AI work together best. Read this before executing any skill or communicating with Olly.

---

## Programme Design & Delivery Sessions

- Ask one question at a time. Olly explicitly asked to slow down mid-session when multiple questions were asked at once — this is a firm preference, not a style choice.
- When programme structure is not yet defined, move through it one element at a time: duration → stages → calls → pricing → community → what's next. Never front-load.
- Always confirm tiers before documenting pricing. Ask "is there more than one tier?" before writing any pricing section. Programmes may have a 1-2-1 and a DIY option with different inclusions and prices.
- Confirm decisions out loud before moving on: "So the structure is X — does that feel right?" Catching corrections early saves rework.
- At the end of every design session, update the relevant delivery system doc and priorities.md before closing.

## YouTube Long-Form Content Creation

- Work through one field at a time in conversation — title first, then hook, then the rest
- Always present the full drafted entry for review before pushing to Notion
- For title work: present 3 options, discuss, refine until Olly lands on the one he wants — don't push until confirmed
- Maximum 9 words per title
- Once Olly says "go for it" or equivalent — push immediately without re-asking
- After pushing, bring the next video title into conversation straight away
