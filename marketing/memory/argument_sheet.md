# Argument Sheet — Olly Henson Coaching

> The strategic spine of all content. Every piece of marketing — regardless of format or platform — must connect to at least one stage of this argument. Read this before writing anything.

---

## What We Do

We help people open their hearts so they can embody and feel their future as real, so it happens.

**The subconscious reprogramming is the mechanism, not the destination.** Never lead with the mechanism — lead with the life they want to create.

---

## The core argument in one line

**It's not the technique — it's the state.**

The techniques work. Visualisation, affirmations, Dispenza meditations, Neville Goddard — they all work. But only when done from the right internal state. The reason nothing is changing is not a technique problem or a consistency problem. It is a state problem. A closed heart and a nervous system stuck in survival keeps people in their analytical mind, blocks access to the subconscious, and makes it impossible to genuinely feel the emotional states needed for change. The Heart-Opening Meditation solves this. It opens the heart, shifts the nervous system out of survival, and makes genuine feeling possible. That is when the techniques begin to work.

---

## Niche

**Reality creation, manifestation and subconscious reprogramming.** The primary audience is people actively trying to create a new reality — health, wealth, relationships, identity — who understand the theory but cannot bridge the gap to lived experience. The neuroscience, HeartMath research, and psychology behind the Heart-Opening Meditation give this positioning scientific credibility beyond typical manifestation content. The manifestation world (Abraham Hicks, Neville Goddard, Dispenza followers) is the primary crossover audience — they are already sold on the concept of creating reality with their mind and looking for a reason why it isn't working.

---

## Audience Consciousness Level

The ideal 1-2-1 client sits between Courage (200) and Love (500) on David Hawkins' Map of Consciousness. Speak across the full band — primary focus Neutrality through Reason (250–400), touching Courage to meet those ready to act and Love to call forward those sensing something larger. Content is emotional but not pain-focused. The frustration is at the mechanics — why isn't this working — not at themselves. Personal pain (the recognition that time has passed without change) is used sparingly for maximum impact. Lead with emotional recognition, follow with intellectual precision. Energising and forward-facing throughout.

## Promised Land

Able to comfortably embody and feel the future that they want to create until it happens.

Their specific creation goal may be:

### The Specific Identities They're Moving Into

These are not abstract — they are named, felt identities the prospect can picture. Make the Promised Land concrete by naming one of these in hooks and scripts.

**The Person Who Finally Feels It**
They've said the affirmations, done the visualisations, read the books — but they've never truly felt like the person they're trying to become. The Promised Land is that moment when the feeling is real. Not imagined. Not performed. Felt.

**The Person Who Creates Consistently**
Not someone who gets inspired for three days then crashes back. Someone who can access elevated emotional states on demand, stay in them, and watch their outer world begin to shift as a result.

**The Person Who Shows Up**
Keeps plans. Stays for the whole party. Attends events feeling well and present. Shows up for family, work, and life without guilt or apology. *"My son is getting married in October and I want to be well and happy for it."* — Elizabeth

**The Person Who Moves Freely**
Walks, gardens, gets back on the court. Moves through the world without calculating the cost of every activity. *"I would like to get back to playing my favourite sports."* — Isabell

**The Person With a Clear Head**
Thinks sharply. Makes decisions without fog. Concentrates. No longer hijacked by overthinking.

**The Person Who Wakes Up Ready**
First thought in the morning is not about what might go wrong, how bad the symptoms are, or whether today will be another wasted day. Starts with genuine energy and quiet confidence.

**The Old Happy Me**
Not a new, improved version — the one they already were before everything got complicated. Light, soft, present, steady. Enjoying life rather than enduring it. *"I know the old happy me is still inside me somewhere."* — Elizabeth

## The Differentiation

Most creators in this niche talk about the brain, the subconscious mind, and the nervous system. Olly's content revolves around the heart — its science, its misconceptions, and its sacred meaning.

This is the core differentiator. The heart has its own nervous system. It sends more signals to the brain than the brain sends to it. It produces an electromagnetic field measurable several feet outside the body. Every ancient culture placed it at the centre of human experience. And yet most people have been taught it is merely a pump — and that real intelligence lives in the head.

Olly's content systematically dismantles this. It is eye-opening, highly shareable, and resonant for an audience that has tried to think their way to a new life and discovered it doesn't work.

**Content rule:** Always connect back to the heart. Not just as the mechanism — as the destination.

## Aspirational Identity — The Calling Forward

The second framing is more powerful for the Dispenza/Neville/Abraham Hicks audience — people who are not just seeking relief, but actively pursuing transformation. They already believe. They are looking for someone who can make it real.
