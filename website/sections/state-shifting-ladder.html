State Shifting Ladder

Use this emotional scaling ladder to elevate your state out of survival and put yourself into creative mode.

You can use this before any creative practice or simply to elevate your state during your day.

Either start with the level that you feel you're at right now or work your way from the bottom to the top.

The higher the level you reach the more creative power you gain.

This tool supports everyday shifts in emotional state. It isn't therapy or crisis support. Nothing you do here is stored, tracked, or shared — everything happens in your browser and your data isn't used in any way.
